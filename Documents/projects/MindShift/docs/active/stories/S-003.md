## Vision

Split MindShift into two distinct but complementary flows, accessible from a redesigned landing page.

**Flow 1 — Map My Future**: A guided 8-step wizard asking about each life aspect in 5 years (career, creativity, health, relationships, travel, finances, living situation + synthesis sentence). The answers build a personalised mind map canvas with recommendations per node. From any expanded node the user can apply a historical lens to that specific aspect.

**Flow 2 — Shift My Perspective**: A lighter, faster path. The user vents about something on their mind — a decision, a fear, a frustration — then chooses a historical figure lens to get a reframe. No mind map, no wizard, just raw input → perspective shift.

Both flows share the lens library (page 4) and the perspective output (page 5). The landing page (page 1) is the fork.
